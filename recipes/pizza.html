Pizza

Description

I cooka da pizza

Pizza n pasta

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzerella
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Stretch out the pizza dough into a circle.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce on it evenly.
  3. Put on the mozzerella evenly across the sauce.
  4. Put on the pepperoni to your desired amount.
  5. Put in the oven for 999F for 50 minutes.
